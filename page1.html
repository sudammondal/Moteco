Tactical Communication Systems

Today's soldiers need multi-functional, reliable and rugged equipment designed for performance and flexibility on the front line. Tactical mobiles are complete integrated systems tailored to customer requirements. They consist of heavy duty 4x4 trucks or trailers that house a large communications shelter that can include VSAT, TETRA base stations, LTE base stations, Wireless Routers, telescopic masts, Power Generators and HVAC systems.
